Career Info

Robert Downey Jr is an American actor, producer, and singer. He began acting at the age of 5 in his father Robert Downey Sr’s film Pound. Then as a teenager becoming a member of the Brat Pack working in many popular teen films. His breakout role won him an Oscar nomination and BAFTA for Best Actor playing the title role in Chaplin.

The late 90s and early 2000s Downey Jr had multiple run-ins with the law stemming from drug issues. After several court ordered stints in rehab from 2001-2003, Downey has maintained sobriety since then. Friend and fellow actor Mel Gibson paid his insurance bonds in 2003 allowing Downey to star in many films spanning comedy and drama.He’s most known for his role as Tony Stark/Ironman within the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

From 2013-2015 Forbes listed Downey Jr as Hollywood’s highest paid actor. His films have grossed over $14.4 billion worldwide, making him the second highest grossing box-office star of all time.
